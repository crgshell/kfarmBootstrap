About Konocti Farms

Our proprietary cannabis strains are specifically designed to maximize a variety of microclimates found in Northern California Appellations.
We understand the concept of Terroir.
Terroir can be very loosely translated as "a sense of place". Just like wine, coffee, tobacco and hops.
Cannabis genetics interact with a specific set of geographic, geologic and climate variables, to express a unique agricultural product.
Our Process is strictly organic.
We never use chemical Fertilizers, PESTICIDES: Insecticides, Rodenticides & Herbicides.
It's true that our proven organic process is more labor-intensive and costly.
However we know through experience we produce a superior product.
Protecting the environment, good land stewardship is something we're passionate about!

Cooperatives

Individual patients and cooperatives can apply for collective membership. A membership agreement is required to conform with California medical cannabis laws. A membership agreement is a contract between the patient or cooperative and the collective. The membership agreement is the patients commitment to abide by all requirements laid out in the bylaws of the collective. All patients must have a valid doctors recommendation to become a member of the collective and receive medical cannabis deliveries.
